Research Interests

(1) Soil physics/vadose zone hydrology/Ecohydrology.

(2) Fate and transport of mass and energy in cold and arid regions.

(3) Soil and water conservation and climate change.

(4)Instrumentation (e.g., heat pulse probe, thermo-TDR, thermos-FDR, TDR/FDR, DTS, cosmic-ray probe, GPR, ERT) and software development.
